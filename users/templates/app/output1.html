Educational Loan Information

What is an Educational Loan?

An educational loan is a financial assistance provided by banks or financial institutions to students who need funds to pursue higher education. It covers tuition fees, books, accommodation, and other academic expenses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Admission to a recognized university or institution.
  • Good academic record and co-borrower (parent/guardian) support.
  • Age limit: 18 - 35 years (varies by bank).

Interest Rates

Interest rates for educational loans range from **6.5% to 12%**, depending on the loan amount and repayment tenure.

Documents Required

  • Admission letter from a recognized university.
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN Card).
  • Address proof.
  • Bank statements of the parent/guardian.
  • Income proof of the co-borrower.

Repayment & Benefits

Repayment starts after **6 months to 1 year** of course completion. The loan tenure usually ranges from **5 to 15 years**.

  • Tax benefits under Section 80E.
  • No collateral required for loans up to ₹7.5 lakhs.
  • Subsidized interest rates for economically weaker sections.
