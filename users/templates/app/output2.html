Home Loan Information

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be at least 21 years old at the time of loan application and not more than 65 years at loan maturity.
  • Minimum monthly income should be above ₹30,000 (may vary as per lender's policy).
  • Employment: Salaried, self-employed professionals, or business owners with stable income sources.
  • Good credit score (preferably above 750) with a clean repayment history.
  • Applicant should have a minimum of 2 years of work experience or business continuity.

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License.
  • Address Proof: Utility Bills, Rental Agreement, Passport, or Driving License.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Last 3 months’ salary slips, Form 16, and last 6 months’ bank statements.
    • Self-employed: Last 3 years’ ITR, Profit & Loss Account, Balance Sheet, and Bank Statements.
  • Property Documents: Sale Agreement, Title Deed, Approved Building Plan, and NOC from the builder/society.
  • Photographs: Passport-sized recent photographs.

Interest Rates & Loan Tenure

Interest rates range from 6.5% to 9.5% per annum, depending on the applicant’s profile, credit score, and market conditions. Loan tenure options are flexible, ranging from 5 years to 30 years.

Loan Features & Benefits

  • Up to 90% financing of property value based on eligibility.
  • Balance transfer facility to reduce interest burden.
  • Top-up loan option available.
  • Quick processing and minimal documentation.
  • Prepayment and foreclosure options without extra charges (conditions apply).

Types of Home Loans

  • Home Purchase Loan: For buying a new or resale residential property.
  • Home Construction Loan: For constructing a new home on own plot.
  • Home Improvement Loan: For renovation, repair, or extension of existing house.
  • Home Loan Balance Transfer: To transfer an existing loan to another lender at lower interest rates.
  • Top-Up Loan: Additional loan amount over and above the existing home loan.

Additional Charges

  • Processing Fee: 0.25% to 1% of loan amount (may vary by bank).
  • Legal & Technical Charges.
  • Prepayment/Foreclosure Charges (only in specific cases).
  • Stamp Duty & Registration Charges (for property purchase).

How to Apply?

  1. Check your loan eligibility and calculate EMI using an online calculator.
  2. Choose the loan amount and tenure as per your repayment capacity.
  3. Submit the application form with necessary documents.
  4. Wait for document verification and property evaluation.
  5. Once approved, sign the agreement and receive the sanctioned amount.

Important Tips

  • Maintain a good credit score for better interest rates.
  • Compare loan offers from multiple banks before finalizing.
  • Read all terms and conditions carefully.
  • Opt for affordable EMIs to avoid financial stress.
